# **App Name**: TechFix Express

## Core Features:

- Homepage & Call to Action: A welcoming landing page featuring key business information and clear calls to action to engage visitors, with a prominent title reflecting home appliance repair.
- Services/Products Listing: Dedicated sections for clearly presenting the business's appliance repair offerings with descriptive text and accompanying imagery.
- About Us & Business Profile: An informative page detailing the business's story, mission, values, and team members to build trust and connection within the home appliance repair industry.
- Contact Information & Form: An easily accessible contact section including business hours, address (if applicable), phone, email, and an interactive submission form for inquiries.
- Portfolio / Gallery: A visual showcase section where the business can display their successful repair projects or relevant appliance images in an organized and attractive manner.
- Customer Testimonials Display: A dedicated section to feature positive client feedback and reviews, enhancing credibility and social proof for the business's repair services.
- AI Business Text Tool: A built-in generative AI tool to assist the business owner in quickly creating marketing content such as slogans, short product descriptions, or 'About Us' paragraphs based on user prompts, specifically tailored for appliance repair services.
- Interactive Inquiry Form: An interactive form for customers to submit details about their appliance repair needs, including appliance type, model, and symptoms of malfunction or breakdown, displayed on the page with Hebrew labels.

## Style Guidelines:

- A sophisticated blue-grey as the primary color (#556B80), chosen to convey professionalism and reliability, evoking a modern, yet calm aesthetic, suitable for a repair service.
- A very light, almost white background color (#F2F4F6), visually aligned with the primary hue but heavily desaturated to ensure excellent readability and a clean feel, especially for detailed forms and Hebrew text.
- A brighter, more saturated analogous accent color (#85CFE2), serving to highlight interactive elements, calls to action, and key information, providing a refreshing contrast for service requests.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, highly legible, and objective characteristics, suitable for conveying professional business information clearly across all devices, with full support for Hebrew characters.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist line icons that align with the professional and modern aesthetic of the website, enhancing navigation and visual understanding without clutter, including icons for various appliances.
- Emphasize a clean, modular layout with generous whitespace to ensure content is easily digestible and the overall design feels uncluttered and professional, particularly for the inquiry form and right-to-left Hebrew layout.
- Use the provided image of a technician repairing a washing machine as the main background for the entire page, ensuring it complements content without distracting from it and sets the theme of appliance repair.
- Implement subtle and elegant animations, such as gentle fades or slides for section transitions and hover effects, to provide a refined and engaging user experience without being distracting during form submission.
